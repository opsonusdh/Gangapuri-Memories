Santanu Ghatak

Santanu Ghatak, the English teacher who is about as chill as a teacher can be. Most of the time, he’s friendly, approachable, and one of the few teachers students can actually talk to without feeling like they’re facing a court trial. Because of that, he’s earned a level of respect that can’t be forced by authority alone.

That said, student folklore insists that when Santanu Sir gets angry, he transforms from a calm English teacher into a heavyweight boxing champion. According to legend, anyone on the receiving end of his wrath feels less like a student and more like a punching bag in a training session. Fortunately, these stories are far more famous than the actual incidents.

What makes him unique is that, despite all the exaggerated tales, students rarely fear him. His friendly nature always wins in the end, making him one of the more well-liked teachers in the school. Of course, being popular comes with a price: somewhere along the way, a few students decided that “Santanu” was too ordinary and began calling him “Santo Nunu”Relaxed Penis instead. The nickname spread faster than exam rumors and has since become part of Gangapuri folklore.
