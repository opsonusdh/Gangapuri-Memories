Ashok

Our assistant headmaster, Ashok sir, is tall, gentle, and always greets you with a friendly smile — the kind that makes you think he’s your best ally.

And he is… unless you’re a commerce student. To them, he’s the ultimate exam villain, a human CCTV camera who can sense cheating attempts before you’ve even unfolded the chit. His long strides across the exam hall feel like the Grim Reaper’s shadow, and his eagle eyes have ended more “group study” plans than the syllabus itself.

According to many whispered tales, Ashok sir is, in fact, a swine also. But whether that’s fair criticism or just the bitter words of students whose chits he confiscated — we’ll let history decide.
